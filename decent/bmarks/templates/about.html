About

"This is my [web bookmarks keeper]. There are many like it, but this one is mine."

tamli is yet another social bookmarking application for storing interesting URLs from around the web.

tamli is open source, so anyone can download the code, modify it, and put it on their own server.

tamli is also federated, which means that any tamli installation can talk to and share bookmarks with any other.

If you don't have a computer of your own that you can use as a server, you can create an account on this tamli installation, just like any other software-as-a-service web application. If you do have a server, though, you can install your own tamli instance and have 100% control of your bookmarks data, while still being able to share bookmarks with your friends, acquaintances, role models, and frenemies via their own tamli instances. Neat, eh?

The name of this application, tamli, is an acronym taken from part of the US Marine Corp Rifleman's Creed.

There are many social bookmarking applications on the web, but this one is mine. And yours too, if you like!
